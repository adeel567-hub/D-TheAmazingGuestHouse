Our Room Features and Location

Our Rooms

We got Single, Double and Family type Rooms all rooms having modern facilities.The room size is just perfect according to its type. Our room facilities consists of food ordering, WiFi(24 hours), Telephone Handset with direct access to Reception(24 hours), Coffee and Tea making facilities, Attached bath, Flat screen T.V, Laundry and Ironing Service is available on request with a small charge.

Our Location

We are based in the heart of the ever pleasing and beautiful town of Weston Super Mare. The train station is around 1 mile and the shopping centre and sea front is with in walking distance of less than a mile. We also provide pick and drop facilities to both train station and nearest airport on reasonable charges. Please be notified that pick and drop facilities have to be booked with at least 2 hours in advance. We are located on a very quiet road so your stay will be very comfortable yet exciting with us.
